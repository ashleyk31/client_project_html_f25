UMSI Career Exploration Page

What is Career Exploration?

Career exploration is the process of learning more about your interests, values, and skills while investigating different industries and career paths. At UMSI, students are encouraged to use resources, events, and mentorship opportunities to better understand where their degree can take them.

Campus Involvement

Getting involved on campus is one of the best ways to explore potential career paths. Student organizations, workshops, and leadership opportunities help you practice professional skills, expand your network, and learn more about your areas of interest. This Conference Prep Workshop document will prepare you to make meaningful and long-lasting connections before and during conference you are attending.

Conferences and Networking

Conferences allow you to meet professionals in the field, gain insight into industry trends, and practice introducing yourself in a professional setting. The UMSI Career Development Conference Prep and Networking guide provide strategies for reaching out to alumni, preparing your elevator pitch, and following up with new contacts.
